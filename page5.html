Local Events

Everyone cares about what's happening in their area. Whether they have a family and weekend events to plan, or are looking for a great date night, compelling and interesting local events can increase the social value of all your info. Bonus points if you find something your followers want to share with potential event partners in crime.

DIY Projects

We live in a Pinterest world where some of the most shared content are videos depict everyday people doing extraordinary or interesting things. Weekend projects for the crafty (or just those dreaming on Pinterest boards) can help bring your brand into peoples homes.

Interesting Home Trends

Most people use social media to stay in the know, and everyone wants to be the first person to share and discover the latest trend...and home owners and buyers are no different. Discovering the latest paint technique or reclaimed fixture craze brings some serious social clout to you (and your followers).

Exciting Local News

Is your city building a new open air mall? Have an Amazon store planned in the future? Or even better is a new tech-heavy housing development planned later this year? You should know the answer to all these questions, and better still, so should you potential clients. Finding out the latest in neighbord renovations, city ordinances, and restaurant openings is just a small part of being a local expert. Sharing it with your social media followers...is just fun!

Local Reviews

Every client wants to hire a Realtor with some serious inside knowledge. A buyer wants to know that you're going to show them the best "little known" developments around town, and can help point them in the direction of great local ammenities. A seller wants you to know every detail of the city that can impact the price of their home. Reviewing local businesses on services like Yelp, Google, and your very own blog can go a long way to increasing not only your SEO...but your status as a local expert!
