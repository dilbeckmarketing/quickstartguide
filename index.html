ADDING CONTACTS TO THE DILBECK CRM

Your contacts are the life blood of your CRM! Learn how to import leads in bulk to the CRM, manage and create lists, or import directly from your Gmail account.

ADDING & MANAGING LEADS IN THE DILBECK CRM

Leads and contacts act a bit differently in the CRM. Leads can only be claimed by one agent systemwide and have a bunch of fun features marketing contacts can't boast.

CUSTOMIZING & SENDING EMAIL TEMPLATES

There are a ton of pre-made emails ready to be sent in the CRM right now, and even more you can create and customize. Learn how to edit and personalize existing templates and whip up a few of your own.

SETTING UP DRIP CAMPAIGNS

Drip campaigns are emails designed to be set predetermined intervals to a chosen contact list. The CRM has three, 12 month drip campaigns ready to be sent out to your sphere.

CUSTOMIZING WEBSITE & MENU

Stand out in the crowd with customized homepage photos, videos, menu options and more. Your homepage is going to be the first impression some potential clients have of you, make it zing!

COMPLETING ACCOUNT PROFILE & INTEGRATIONS

There are a ton of ingegrations, profile options, social media settings, and traffic tracking options ready to make your site turbo charged. Don't forget to link your social media and MLS accounts.

CREATING CUSTOM CONTENT PAGES

A website is nothing without content! Keyword rich headings can help increase your Google search ranking and good local or industry specific content can help increase your websites value to visitors and potential clients. Find out how to make pages with everything from video to written content.

CREATING CUSTOM SEARCH PAGES

Want to showcase you listed or sold properties or create pages specializing in historic or unique properties? Looking to share some potential income properties to clients or on social? Custom search pages can help populate a page with listings based on specified criteria!


ATTACHING AREA PROFILES

Area profiles offer a quick and comprehensive overview of areas, cities, and neighborhoods. From entertainment to the latest market insights, these pages are crafted to keep your clients in the know. Dilbeck currently has 22 pre-made area profiles ready and waiting to be attached to your agent site.

CREATING CUSTOM AREA PROFILES

Hyper-local marketing is an amazing approach for agents! You can create custom area profiles for choice cities, neighborhoods, and even housing tracts to showcase your local expertise and become a valuable local resource for potential clients.

SOCIAL MEDIA GUIDES

Social media marketing can seem as intimidating as it is necessary for modern marketing plans. Don't sweat the small stuff and find out how to get started mastering your social media channels, and more importantly how to spend your time on social media wisely.

SOCIAL MEDIA POSTS & COVER PHOTOS

A social media account with content is a sad, sad thing. All platforms favor pages and accounts that post regularly and include content they think will be valuable for their visitors. Find some customized Facebook covers and social media graphics to get your posting schedule started!
